Our Services

We offer a variety of services to enhance your experience:

  • Book Lending: Borrow books for a specified period. Return them on time to avoid late fees.
  • Reference Services: Get assistance in finding resources and information for your research.
  • Study Rooms: Reserve study rooms for individual or group study sessions.
  • Events and Workshops: Participate in events, workshops, and book clubs organized by the library.
  • Online Resources: Access e-books, journals, and databases from anywhere.
